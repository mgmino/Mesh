Search metacharacters

field:search specific field
.match any character
?match zero or one
*match zero or more
+match one or more
{n}match n times
{m,n}match m through n times
{n,}match n or more times
^beginning of string
$end of string
[[:<:]]match beginning of words
[[:>:]]match ending of words
[:class:]match a character class
alnum - alphanumeric
alpha - letters
blank - whitespace
digit - number
lower - lowercase
punct - punctuation
space - space, \t, \n, \r
upper - uppercase
[abc]match one of enclosed chars
[a-c]match any char in range
[^xyz]match any char not enclosed
|separates alternatives
//escape special characters
